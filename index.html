How to Use TypeRacer

Welcome to TypeRacer!

TypeRacer is a typing speed test application that measures your words per minute (WPM) accuracy.

Getting Started:
  1. Select Difficulty: Choose between Easy, Medium, or Hard from the dropdown menu.
  2. Read the Text: A sample text will appear in the blue box above the input field.
  3. Start Typing: Simply begin typing the sample text. The timer will automatically start on your first keystroke.
  4. Stop the Test: Press Enter to complete the test and see your results.
  5. Retry: Click the Retry button to reset and try again with a new text sample.
How Results are Calculated:
  • Time: Measured in seconds from your first keystroke to when you press Enter.
  • Correct Words: Words you typed exactly matching the sample text are counted as correct.
  • WPM: Words Per Minute is calculated by taking the number of correct words divided by the time in minutes.
Tips for Better Scores:
  • Focus on accuracy first, speed will follow naturally.
  • Keep your hands in proper typing position on the keyboard.
  • Practice regularly to improve both speed and accuracy.
  • Use the correct fingers for each key to develop muscle memory.
